What Is Game Development?

Game development is the process of creating interactive digital experiences. It combines art, storytelling, programming, sound, and user experience design. Some games are made by huge teams, while others are created by a single independent developer.

A successful game usually requires clear goals, strong mechanics, and an engaging visual style. Developers must also test their work repeatedly to fix bugs and improve the overall player experience.

“Games are the most elevated form of investigation.” — Albert Einstein
