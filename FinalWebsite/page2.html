Game Design

Game design focuses on rules, mechanics, objectives, and player choices. Designers decide how players interact with the world, what goals they pursue, and what makes the experience enjoyable.

Strong design creates a meaningful gameplay loop. This loop is the repeated pattern of actions a player performs, such as exploring, collecting rewards, and upgrading abilities.

Important Parts of Game Design

  • Core gameplay loop
  • Challenge and reward balance
  • Level progression
  • Player feedback
Good game design gives players meaningful choices and clear feedback.
